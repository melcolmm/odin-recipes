Air Fryer Ranch Pork Chops

Description

This air fryer pork chop recipe is so simple with ranch seasoning mix and just 10 minutes in the air fryer. I prefer my chops slightly pink to ensure juiciness, but feel free to add an extra minute on each side for well-done. Try different varieties of ranch mix (like spicy or bacon) to change up the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • cooking spray
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressing mix

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place pork chops onto a plate and lightly spray both sides with cooking spray.
  3. Sprinkle both sides with ranch seasoning mix and let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  4. Spray an air fryer basket with cooking spray and preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  5. Working in batches if necessary to ensure they are not overcrowded, place chops into the preheated air fryer. Cook for 10 minutes, flipping halfway through. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
  6. Transfer to a foil-covered plate and let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
